Project Proposal

Implementation of Subscription & Reseller System

Date: April 13, 2026
Ref: ANL-2026-001

1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the implementation of a multi-tier subscription and reseller hierarchy for your analytics platform. The system will enable a scalable business model where clients can manage sub-clients under tiered accounts with automated limits and instant activation.

2. Key Features

  • Reseller Hierarchy: Master accounts can create and oversee sub-clients.
  • Tiered Pricing: Support for custom plans (e.g., ₹100 for 3 account slots).
  • Automated Lifecycle: Instant service activation upon verified purchase.
  • Dynamic Limit Enforcement: Real-time guards to prevent account overages.
